Fitness = checkpoints passed by the best car. ~60 = one full lap.
Generation ends when all cars crash or stall. Best brains are bred & mutated into the next batch. No car survives, only neural net weights carry over.
Mutation = chance that each brain weight changes between generations. Low = fine-tuning, high = wild experiments.
Gen time = max ticks per generation. Increase for longer/harder tracks.
Turn radius = how sharply cars can steer. High = tight turns, low = wide sweeping turns.
